Project Timeline

A history of the Sustainable Transformation Initiative for Land & Learning and its key milestones.

2022

Launch of the STILL initiative. Initial partnerships formed with local communities and research institutions.

2023

First set of environmental sensors deployed. Pilot workshops held for community mapping.

2024

Expansion to 3 new regions. Received major grant for biodiversity tracking.

2025

Dashboard v1.0 released. International conference on Two-Eyed Seeing hosted.

2026

Current phase: Scaling up impact and integrating blockchain for transparent data verification.
