About

Our Story

STILL is dedicated to creating sustainable futures through the integration of Indigenous knowledge systems, innovative economic approaches, and responsible land stewardship.

We believe in building bridges between traditional wisdom and contemporary challenges, fostering solutions that honor both the land and the communities that depend on it.

We walk with the understanding that land is not a resource to be extracted, but a relative to be honoured. Our work grows from listening‚Äîto Elders, to the waters, to the stories written in stone and soil.

In the spirit of Two-Eyed Seeing, we hold Indigenous wisdom in one eye and Western knowledge in the other, using both together for the benefit of all.
