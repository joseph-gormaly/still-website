Sustainable Transformation Initiative for Land & Learning

We bridge the gap between Western science and Indigenous wisdom through Etuaptmumk (Two-Eyed Seeing). Our mission is to foster sustainable relationships with the land and each other.

Our Approach: Two-Eyed Seeing

Indigenous Wisdom

Honouring traditional knowledge systems, oral histories, and deep connection to the land that has sustained communities for millennia.

Western Science

Utilizing data-driven analysis, ecological monitoring, and modern technological tools to understand environmental changes.

The Synthesis

By viewing the world through both lenses simultaneously, we create more holistic, resilient, and effective solutions for the future.
