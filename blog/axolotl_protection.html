Axolotl Protection Initiative

Published on January 22, 2026 by Miguel Torres

The axolotl (Ambystoma mexicanum), a unique amphibian critical to our ecosystem's cultural and biological heritage, faces unprecedented threats. Our new initiative combines ancient chinampa farming techniques with modern water quality monitoring.

The Plan

  1. Habitat Restoration: Cleaning canals and reintroducing native aquatic plants.
  2. Predator Control: Managing populations of invasive fish species that prey on axolotl eggs.
  3. Community Guardians: Training local farmers as custodians of the species.

We believe that by healing the water, we heal ourselves. Join us in protecting this "water monster" and symbol of regeneration.
