Vegetation Change Analysis

Published on January 15, 2026 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Our recent study focuses on the subtle shifts in vegetation patterns across the wetlands. By combining satellite imagery with on-ground Indigenous knowledge, we've identified significant changes in flora distribution over the last decade.

Key Findings

  • Invasive Species: A 15% increase in invasive reed coverage.
  • Native Flora: Resilience observed in traditional medicinal plant zones.
  • Water Quality: Direct correlation between vegetation density and water filtration efficiency.

This research is ongoing, and we invite community members to contribute their observations through our new mobile app.
