The Initiative

About STILL

Sustainable Transformation Initiative for Land & Learning (STILL) is a research initiative focused on developing evidence-based policy frameworks for Indigenous-led land management.

Methodology

Our Approach

We specialize in IPCA governance, GIS analysis, and resource valuation methodologies. Our work bridges academic research with practical implementation, providing measurable outcomes for sustainable land use.

We practice Etuaptmumk (Two-Eyed Seeing)—learning to see from one eye with the strengths of Indigenous ways of knowing, and from the other eye with the strengths of Western ways of knowing, and using both eyes together.

Philosophy

Our Values

"We walk with the understanding that land is not a resource to be extracted, but a relative to be honoured. Our work grows from listening—to Elders, to the waters, to the stories written in stone and soil."

We carry the responsibility passed down through generations: to care for the land so that it may care for those yet to come. All our relations.
